January
185
IG followers
Key wins
IG reach grew from 11 to 791 (72x). Strong story engagement (44 interactions). TikTok launched with 2,691 views & 155 likes. Highest IG engagement rate (17.6%). First followers gained across all platforms.

What didn't work
Low follower conversion on TikTok & Facebook. Zero saves on IG — content not being bookmarked. No DM inquiries tracked. Facebook reach limited (734 views). Low link clicks (2 on IG).

Optimization action
Add strong CTAs to drive saves & link taps. Set up DM inquiry tracking. Boost Facebook content with reels. Introduce property showcase posts to drive saves.
February
300
IG followers
Key wins
Massive Facebook viral reach (220,800 views). TikTok exploded to 10,100 views & 2,400 likes. IG followers grew 79% (+142). TikTok gained 228 new followers. Facebook link clicks hit 2.7k.

What didn't work
IG engagement rate dropped sharply to 1.76% despite follower growth. IG likes fell from 122 to 47 — posts underperformed reels. Stories interactions lower. Reach spike may be one viral post, not consistent.

Optimization action
Prioritize Reels over static posts on IG. Maintain TikTok upload consistency to sustain momentum. Analyze which Feb Facebook post went viral and replicate the format. Add website link to all bio sections.
March
325
IG followers
Key wins
IG engagement rate rebounded to 19.9% (best month). Comments jumped to 18, Shares to 20, Reposts to 25, Saves to 8 — audience more active. TikTok avg watch time improved to 7.05s. Facebook link clicks peaked at 7.3k.

What didn't work
Overall reach dropped significantly on IG (9,528→1,203) and TikTok (10,100→1,900). IG net follower growth turned negative (−2). TikTok completion rate slightly dropped. Facebook views fell sharply (220k→12.6k).

Optimization action
Investigate what drove Feb reach spike and replicate. Use high-engagement content types (Q&As, polls, reels with hooks) to rebuild reach. Run a consistent hashtag strategy. Introduce lead capture (DM funnels, WhatsApp CTA) to convert engaged followers.
